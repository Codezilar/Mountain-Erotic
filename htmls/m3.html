30 Cute Good Morning Texts For Him

Look no further if you need some ideas for cute good morning texts for him. It’s always nice to remind your man of who the most important person in his life is right when he wakes up. If you leave him a good morning text, you are essentially conditioning him to think of you every day as soon as he rises from his slumber. Isn’t it a nice feeling to know that you’re top of mind first thing in the morning? Well, you can enforce that by making it a habit of sending your man sweetly crafted good morning texts.

Don’t worry. You don’t have to be a poet or a skilled writer to send him some cute good morning texts. There’s nothing original under the sun anymore. Feel free to copy some of the best ideas here in this article. Just make sure that you make it your own so that it’s always authentic. Otherwise, it’s only going to sound forced and fake. Here are a few cute good morning texts for him:

1. Good morning! I just wanted to make sure that you start your day with thoughts of me!
2. Hello, my love! I hope that you have the best day today.
3. There are two things that I need to do this morning: have some coffee and tell you that I love you.
4. I’m not having the best morning because you’re not here beside me. But at least I know that I can be the first one you think of when you wake up.
5. Best of mornings to you, my love. Regardless of how things are going to turn out today, know that my love is always there.
6. I just woke up from the most fantastic dream. But real life is much better because you’re here as well.
7. I can say with absolute confidence that I would love nothing more than to wake up in your arms every single day for the rest of my life.
8. At the risk of sounding naughty, I just wanted to let you know that I woke up with the hots for you this morning. Sorry. Can’t help it.
9. I just wanted to take the time to let you know that I woke up this morning with you on my mind. It’s automatic.
10. You’re the man of my dreams and my everyday reality too. Good morning, my love.
11. Your smile is all the sunshine that I need to wake me up in the morning.
12. Hi! I just wanted to take the time this morning to check up on the most handsome guy in the world.
13. Before you start your workday, allow me this opportunity to let you know that I love you.
14. It’s such a privilege to be able to wake up every morning with the chance to let you know that I love you so much.
15. Good morning! If you have some time, come over for breakfast. I have something extra special in mind for you.
16. They say that breakfast is the most important meal of the day. But I still think that nothing beats having you as a snack. Good morning!
17. I’m going to send over some virtual good morning kisses for you now. That’s all I have. I’ll just save the best for later.
18. To be honest, I spent the whole night just dreaming of you. I’m going to spend the rest of the day with you running through my mind.
19. I’ve got nothing creative or mind-blowing to text you this morning. All I can say is that I love you. And I hope that’s enough.
20. Even just the thought of you is enough to bring me a lot of joy and happiness this morning.
21. I may not be waking up with you this morning. But I’m happy knowing that I’ll be seeing you before the day is done.
22. Good morning to the one and only man who makes me feel happy like no other.
23. Good morning, my love! Every day, you steal my heart, and I never want to ask for it back.
24. Is it weird that I went to sleep last night just thinking about how excited I was to text you this morning? That’s the effect you have on me.
25. Hello, my love! I hope your morning coffee is just as hot and steamy as you are.
26. I woke up with the biggest smile on my face because of you. I’m sending you this text in the hopes that you’ll be smiling when you read this too. I love you!
27. By any chance, can I spend every waking morning for the rest of my life with you at my side? I wouldn’t want to have it any other way.
28. It’s like magic having you in my life. That’s the kind of effect that you have on me. And it’s practically the reason why I still face each day with a smile.
29. There’s no way I could ever possibly have a good morning without you in it.
30. I love you. Good morning. Please know that you always have my heart.

Final Thoughts

There you have it. Never underestimate the value of text message communications in a relationship. You must utilize whatever tool you have at your advantage to make sure that your partner always knows how valuable he is to you. A real relationship is always built on healthy and proper communication between two adults. This is the best way to generate intimacy between the two of you.

Intimacy is the level of closeness that you have with one another in your relationship. The closer you are, then the stronger you become as a couple. You might not think much of a text message. But it carries hefty weight in determining the strength of your relationship. So, don’t belittle the value of composing a well-placed good morning message. This can go a very long way in bringing the two of you closer together as a couple.
